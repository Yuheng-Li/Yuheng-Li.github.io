Research Scientist · Adobe Yuheng Li

Hi! I am a research scientist at Adobe Research. Before joining Adobe, I received my PhD in Computer Science from the University of Wisconsin–Madison in 2024, advised by Prof. Yong Jae Lee.

My research spans two main directions: personalized AI systems that leverage user memory for tailored, context‑aware experiences, and controllable generative models, with a recent focus on unified multimodal models and video generation.

Open to collaboration — feel free to reach out.
